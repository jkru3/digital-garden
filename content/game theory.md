game theory is a way of being that has 4 qualities
1. Nice (behave kindly in absence of other information)
	- See [[unconditional love]]
2. Forgiving (do not hold grudges)
	- Not a pushover
	- Not a grudge holder
3. Retaliatory (Provide immediate feedback when harm is issued)
	- Trustworthy and consistent actions and response
4. Clear (obvious about retaliation)
	- Not passive. Effective and observant
