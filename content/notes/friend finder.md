## flow
- flow then asks several questions about a persons
	1. hobbies
	2. values
	3. temperament
- makes an AI "replica"
- people can talk with their own replica. This is important. People should be able to feel confident a model actually represents them
- people should be able to mention controversial ideas. Models should actually represent who they are and what they stand for, and not just a PG version of them.
- system will connect person's replica with other replica's that align most closely

---
## pay features
- different tiers of replicas. Model training is expensive. People can pay for a "deep replica" that will connect them to people with more nuance
- location, age, gender, and language settings paywalled. App connects people with random people, but if they pay for it, they can be more specific about demographics
