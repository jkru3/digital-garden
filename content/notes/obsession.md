The opposite of [[Surrender]] is not only [[resistance]] but [Obsession](https://limna.ai/journal/obsession-or-therapy-the-art-of-repetition.html). And surrender is love, so in obsession, love becomes difficult.

>Obsession is a form of defending against the fear
>If obsession is resistance, what is behind the resistance of it?

Obsession robs us of all happiness and beautiful things

Obsession sells us the idea of a peace that we already have within us

Obsession is the cure that has corrupted into poison

Lest it destroy you, it is important to regulate the intensity of things that are 
1. unimportant
	- See [[Values]]
2. out of your control
	- Idealism (the manifesto), past and future events 
3. are an overreach of [[passion]] 
	- Art, vocation, or obligation that turns into an all consuming prospect 
4. or rob too much intensity from other important things.
	- You can’t practice art all day and be fine

Fear is obsession allowed to act on our thoughts and body. And that fear can distract you from my simple happiness, homework, work, chores, and art. These symptoms are indicative of a force that is antithetical to love.
