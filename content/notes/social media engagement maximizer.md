- You have a top 100 list on twitter
- This is composed of the top 100 people who engage with your content the most
- Every week it looks at your engagement numbers and tells you who you should remove and add in place
- It won't make content for you. It won't magically earn you followers. But it will help you connect with the people you resonate with the most
- This makes it more likely that you and the people you connect with will have a better time engaging with each other
