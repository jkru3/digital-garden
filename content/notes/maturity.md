>maturity is knowing that when your mood is down you should not trust the way you see yourself … If the mind feels stormy, the world will also look gray. When your mood is heavy and down, suspend all self-analysis. When the mind is inclined toward negativity it will not be able to measure your progress well. Wait until the mind is calm to see how far you have really come.
>\-Yung Pueblo
