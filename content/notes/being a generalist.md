a master edger will have his way with the world

society will try very hard to convince you not to edge

but the best place to live is right on the edge. If you live on the edge of town, you live on the boundary that separates humanity from the natural world and reap the blessings of both

you can edge in your business and professional life too. If you work in tech but don’t over-specialize, you will be able to see the needs of adjacent fields and pivot to emerging markets

if you edge, people will wonder how you came to be so creative, happy, and successful in your relationships, friendships, and business dealings

to a master edger, the reason is clear: you are at the corner of everything good, bad, boring, exciting, old, and new. No one could be better suited to be better suited at everything. Whether that is showing love to a child or surviving a street fight
