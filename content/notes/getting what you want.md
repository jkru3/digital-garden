# the secret to getting what you want
some things are impossible to obtain. Some things are close to impossible to obtain. It can be hard to tell the difference, so it’s prudent to assume most things are close to impossible until you get better information.

and if you want to know whether or not it is possible to do something, you may have to want it more than you want to sleep or procrastinate or even be alive. Every moment of idleness has openings. You can only take them if you really want to.

# but what do you want?
most desires are FOMOs

>In their desperate obsession with quality, they compensate by accumulating quantity

when you can’t motivate yourself; dont. Enjoy your time in idleness. Soon you will find your desires are there all along, latent and ready to be activated. All that is needed is to focus on them and trust that they will be activated. There is not any secret to it
