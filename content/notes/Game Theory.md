# the 4 qualities of effective Game Theory
1. nice
	- behave kindly in absence of other information
2. forgiving
	- do not hold grudges
	- not a pushover
	- not a grudge holder
3. retaliatory
	- provide immediate feedback when harm is issued
	- trustworthy and consistent actions and response
5. clear
	- obvious about retaliation
	- not passive. Effective and observant

the outcome of our quality of life is dictated by how we treat others and assert our needs in relationships
