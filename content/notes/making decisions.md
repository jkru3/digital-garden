1. when theres [[Fear]], welcome it
2. when you welcome [[Fear]], get curious about it

if your at a fork in the road, *if you can afford it*, **STOP** and make no decision. The situation will continue to unfold externally and internally.
When fear is manageable, accept it and make your decisions, but *when the fear is a storm*, wait until the fear passes before making a decision

>there’s my business, your business and god’s business. When I’m in your business or god’s business, whose in my business?

# worry
trying to calculate whether or not your decisions have led to the best outcome for yourself isn’t just selfish, it’s self-sabotaging. It infects your mind with an uncertainty that festers until you regret decisions that actually make you happy. Whatever will be, will be. So focus on being happy with where you are and love the people who have chosen to join you
