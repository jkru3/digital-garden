every person is born with a different set of cups
- some people are physically built for certain athletic disciplines
- some people are capable of intellectual genius that others cannot grasp
- some people have the ability understand the needs of others to an unusual depth
we know ‘nurture’ is a large part of our identity. But the cups you and I have only defines our limits. We still have to do the work of filling those cups
