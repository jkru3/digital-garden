i am my own hypnotist: i chose my attitudes, actions, and the outcomes they yield:

(start with a deep 4-count inhale followed by a 6-count exhale)
# attitudes
- i am happy and peaceful
- i am vigorous and mentally sharp
- i see obstacles as opportunities
- i have a propensity for action
# actions
- i observe my reactions and see things as they are in work, rest, meditation and training
- in observation, i bring awareness of the self-inflicted harm of negativity and craving
- to counter this, i wish happiness, peace, and wellness to all beings
- but i remain mindful to direct my focus on what is in my control
# outcomes
- these actions serve to liberate those who are impacted by them
- they protect me from physical harm, manipulation, and destitution
- and they enable me to invite love —in all forms— in abundance into my life
- these outcomes provide me with hope
- hope, which reinforces my attitudes
- attitudes, which determine my actions
- and actions, which influence the state of all things at this moment

(finish with a deep 4-count inhale followed by a 6-count exhale)

may all beings be happy, may all beings be peaceful, may all beings be well
